========================================
COMMON PARENT QUESTIONS
========================================
Q: When does the academic year start?
A: The academic year can begin depending on the child's age and the time of
admission. In line with the Montessori approach, Urvi does not follow a
fixed, documented curriculum calendar.

Q: Can I visit the school before applying?
A: Yes. By scheduling an initial enquiry, you can visit URVI Montessori before,
during, or after school hours. The school can be reached from 8:30 AM to
4:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

Q: What happens if my child is absent?
A: We will notify you about the progress missed that day and the steps to cover
it. Where possible, please inform us in advance if your child will be absent.

Q: How do I schedule a meeting with the Lead Mentor?
A: You can schedule a meeting with the Lead Mentor through our Calendly link,
found in the learning section of the website, where available slots are shown
by day and time.
